Do you want to joint the Mama Garri family?

Team Member

We are currently looking for team members who are full of joy to work on our counters as well as in our kitchens. No previous experience is needed (but it’d be preferable) as full training is provided. We can’t train you to be full of joy, but we can train you how to do the job. We have full time opportunities.


What we are looking for in a Team Member

You’re a positive person, who always looks on the positive side.  
You’re passionate about cooking and hospitality.
You love taking care of yourself and others.
You love food, and love working in a team
You’re over 18 and keep calm under pressure.

Supervisors & Assistants

We are looking for  good natured individuals to come and help us achieve our vision, and help us grow big, strong and popular.

What we are looking for in a Supervisor:
• Energy levels through the roof

• Have a love for developing their teams and focus on unlocking potential.

• Approach each situation to “win” not “fight” .

• Treat each team member as if they were part of your own family

• They live and breathe the Mama Garri values and drive this culture throughout their teams and restaurants.
